What Is Covid-19

Coronavirus

Coronavirus disease (COVID-19) is an infectious disease caused by the SARS-CoV-2 virus. Most people infected with the virus will experience mild illness and recover without requiring special treatment. Older people and those with underlying medical conditions like cardiovascular disease, diabetes, chronic respiratory disease, or cancer are more likely to develop serious illness. The best way to prevent it is to be well informed about the disease.

Contagion

Coronavirus can be transmitted via many routes, mostly when people breathe in air contaminated by droplets and small airborne particles containing the virus.

Air Transmission

When an infected person coughs, sneezes, the droplets carry the virus into the air from their nose or mouth causing transmission

Human Contacts

With close human proximity, the virus can be transmitted resulting to a large-scale spread of the disease

Contaminated Objects

Corona virus can stay on surfaces for many days. Clean the objects before using them

What should we do

Following the precautionary measures can greatly reduce the chance of getting infected with coronavirus.

01 Wear Masks

Masks should be used when in public areas to prevent air transmission of the virus. Clean your hands before you put your mask on, as well as after you take it off. Make sure it covers both your nose, mouth and chin

02Wash Your Hands

Handwashing with soap remains one of our best defenses against the virus. It is of critical importance especially when outdoors. Handwashing after touching common surfaces such as doorknobs or handles will keep ourselves and others around us safe.

03Use nose - rag

Cover your nose and mouth with a tissue while sneezing and coughing. Dispose the tissue after use. Stopping the spread is crucial!

04Avoid contacts

COVID-19 mostly spreads through human contact i.e. if some having the virus sneezes, coughs or is near you. Initiate human contact only when necessary. Maintain Social Distancing because Prevention is the key!!
